LLMs, Prompt Engineering & AI Concepts

1.  What are LLMs and how they process text

LLMs (Large Language Models) are AI systems trained on large amounts of
text data to predict the next token in a sequence.

How they work: - Input text is split into tokens - Tokens are converted
into numbers - Passed through a Transformer neural network - Model
predicts next token repeatedly to generate output

They do not truly understand language but learn statistical patterns.

2.  Tokens and Context Window

Tokens: Small pieces of text such as words, subwords, or characters.

Example: “I love AI” → [“I”, ” love”, ” AI”]

Context Window: The maximum number of tokens the model can process at
once. If exceeded, older parts of the input are forgotten.
